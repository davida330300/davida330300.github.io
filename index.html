Project

Market Trend Prediction Model & Mutual Funds Investment (Acadimic Sinica)

Our paper: Auto-labeling and Learning Market bull/bear Trend Prediction Model with Applications in Investing Mutual Funds. Previous studies primarily focus on one-day-ahead stock prediction. However, a longer market prediction model is also crucial for many applications such as fund investments. In this paper, a monthly market prediction model using macroeconomic variables is proposed. We use the graph attention network to determine the importance of each macroeconomic variable. It provides an interpretable result for our model. We also propose the label-lagging problem occurring when training a supervised market trend prediction model in real world. Empirically, we show that our proposed method is robust to this problem. We also examine our method by investing in the fund market with some trading strategies. Our method can achieve a promising result compared to nowadays other methods.
